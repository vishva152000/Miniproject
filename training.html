Training


As experts in Management Standards Training, QSys Management Systems offers the best-in-class Training Programs that can help you stay up-to-date with the ever-evolving system practices, regulatory and technical standards.


QSys Management Systems works with experienced practitioners, who train you with real-world insights following a participatory approach.


Choose from a suite of Courses- Foundation /Awareness Modules and internal Audit Courses that adhere to the specified training requirements.


Delivery Options


On-Site


QSys Management Systems can structure training delivery models or bespoke modules and deliver it in-house. The delivery models can be tailored to your organization in actual settings enabling first-hand application while learning. This form of training is not only perfect to ensure delivery of proprietary and confidential information but also helps in cost savings and minimizes disruption to save time.


You can also run two-three programs at the same time; conduct half day seminars or multi-day workshops.


Customized Training


QSys Management Systems customizes training programs to meet your unique requirements to reinforce your messages critical to your organizational culture.
