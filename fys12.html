BSCI


BSCI stands for Business Social Compliance Initiative. It is a leading business-driven drive for companies committed to improving working conditions in the global supply chain.

A European social monitoring system for ethical sourcing, it was initiated by the Brussels-based Foreign Trade Association (FTA).

Following dramatic growth of BSCI over the years, in 2011 FTA made the decision to clearly include sustainability as one of its pillars and therefore adopted a stronger organizational structure to further develop the growth of the association and maintain an excellent service to its members.

BSCI unite more than 900 companies around a development-oriented system applicable to all sectors and sourcing countries.

The BSCI code of conduct covers the following areas


>> Legal compliance

>> Working hours

>> Compensation

>> Prohibition of child labor

>> Prohibition of forced labor and disciplinary measures

>> Freedom of association and the right to collective bargaining

>> Prohibition of all forms of discrimination

>> Workplace health and safety

>> Management systems

>> Environment and safety issues
